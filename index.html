LooxOS

LooxOS is an Web-OS forked from OrchidOS. This OS is higly customizable it can be installed on PC (Laptop, and desktop), Phone and Tablet (Android, This might not be possible on every phone due to specific brand restriction, and SoC linux compatibility), also a Smart TV version but for computer and embedded devices.
